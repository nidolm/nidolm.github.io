Our Theme Song - "La Herida"

Jasper MC Solilap


The song me and Vlad chosed "Le Herida", or "The Wounded" to be our Ignatian prayer buddy theme song. Cristóbal Fones, SJ. with lyrics by José María Rodríguez Olaizola, SJ, the song was translated into English and French by Phillip Hurley, SJ, and Pierre Bélanger, SJ, respectively.

The lyrics "WITH A FIRE OF HOPE WE ARE IN MOTION, HUMANITY WITH LOVE DIVINE UNITING" reflect the Ignatian spirit of hope, love, and unity in the Jesuit context. The Jesuit tradition emphasizes the importance of men and women working for and with others for the greater good and the service of humanity. The line "WITH A FIRE OF HOPE WE ARE IN MOTION" can be interpreted as a call to action inspired by the Ignatian faith's hope and the desire to make a positive difference in the world. "HUMANITY WITH LOVE DIVINE UNITING" embodies the Jesuit value of love and solidarity, seeking to unite all people in God's love and the advancement of justice and peace.
