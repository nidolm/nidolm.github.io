Code SPARK

Jasper MC Solilap

As an Ateneean with my prayer buddy Vlad, living out the principles of Code SPARK can significantly impact our academic and personal lives.

  1. Self-Giving and Self-Donation: Self-giving and self-donation can be exemplified through acts of generosity and volunteering, as well as "tutulong kahit walang kapalit." In our own experience, Vlad and I showed this by helping and supporting each other, such as exchanging ideas through study sessions and offering assistance to classmates, and together, we are one.

  2. Primacy on Prayer: Taking time to pray and seeking guidance can help us navigate challenges and maintain balance during the semester. Vlad and I, nevertheless our Religion, show respect to one another because Jesus taught us to love and be kind to one another regardless of their Religion, colour, or financial status.

  3. Authenticity in Life: Embracing authenticity as an Atenean involves staying true to our values, beliefs, and goals. We pursue genuinely interesting subjects like ASF, expressing our ideas openly and engaging in honest communication with peers and instructors (Thank you, Teacher Nanan).

  4. Responsibility: Being a Responsible student is challenging for our academic endeavors. Such encompasses meeting deadlines, fulfilling commitments, and being accountable for our actions. To do this, Vlad and I have an agreement. This includes managing our time effectively, completing assignments with diligence, and acknowledging the impact of our choices on our academic journey in ASF and other subjects.

  5. Kindness and Love: We demonstrate compassion and love as an Atenean by fostering positive relationships with peers, not just peers, but with our professors, teachers, and elders. Showing empathy towards others' (Like serving in the orphanage) face the challenges, promoting an inclusive and supportive learning environment. Jesus told us that no matter what happens, remember to "LOVE OTHERS AS WE LOVE OURSELVES. " With this perspective, we can show the world that even though it is cruel and unfair, kindness and love still win.
