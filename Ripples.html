"DO NOT FEAR FOR I AM WITH YOU, DO NOT BE AFRAID FOR I AM YOUR GOD. I WILL STRENGTHEN YOU, I WILL HELP YOU, I WILL UPHOLD YOU, WITH MY VICTORIOUS RIGHT HAND."
- ( Isaiah 41:10)

As Isaiah said, Vlad, my prayer buddy, helped and upheld each other to emphasize the importance of prayer, discernment, and finding God in all things. In the context of Ignatian spirituality, having my Vlad or prayer buddy can be seen as a manifestation of the support and companionship that God provides to individuals on their spiritual journey. The verse from Isaiah 41:10 reassures we believers should not fear because God is with us, strengthening and upholding us throughout our journey. When we applied the concept of having a prayer buddy in Ignatian spirituality, Vlad and I underscored that we can find strength and comfort in faith through mutual support and prayerful companionship.
